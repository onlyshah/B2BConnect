# B2BConnect Platform - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Executive Summary

B2BConnect is an **End-to-End B2B Commerce, Distribution, Sales Force Automation, Product Discovery, Advertising, and Retail Engagement Platform** that digitizes the entire supply chain connecting Companies (Manufacturers/Brands), Distributors, Salesmen, and Retailers on a single digital ecosystem.

**Target Market:** FMCG (Soaps, Household Products, Beverages, Cosmetics, Grocery)  
**MVP Timeline:** 16 weeks  
**Target Users (Year 1):** 500+ Companies, 5,000+ Distributors, 10,000+ Salesmen, 100,000+ Retailers

---

## Problem Statement

### Current State (Without B2BConnect)
- **Companies:** Manual distributor management, no visibility into field activities, delayed order processing
- **Distributors:** Paper-based inventory, manual order entry, no retailer insights, fragmented payment collection
- **Salesmen:** No digital tools, manual forms, lost data, no performance tracking
- **Retailers:** No product discovery, manual ordering, no credit information, no standardized communication

### Pain Points
1. **Fragmented Communication** - Multiple channels, phone calls, no record
2. **Data Loss** - Paper forms, spreadsheets, inconsistent data
3. **Delayed Intelligence** - No real-time visibility into sales/inventory
4. **No Standardization** - Each company operates differently
5. **Limited Reach** - Small retailers can't discover products
6. **No Performance Metrics** - Salesmen unmotivated, distributors underperforming

## Core Platform Features

### 1. **Story Feed System** (All Apps)
- **Content Types:** Product launches, promotional videos, training materials, competitor alerts
- **Analytics:** Views, clicks, CTR, orders generated from stories
- **Targeting:** By region, distributor, product category, retailer type
- **Timeline:** Schedule posts for automatic publishing

### 2. **Territory Management**
- **Features:** Geographic territories (country, state, city, pincode)
- **Assignment:** Territories assigned to distributors and salesmen
- **Coverage Tracking:** Retailers visited, coverage %, gaps
- **Optimization:** AI-recommended territory adjustments

### 3. **Order Management System**
- **Order Types:**
  - Direct: Retailer → Distributor
  - Salesman-Assisted: Retailer → Salesman → Distributor
- **Order Verification:** Retailer approval for salesman orders
- **Tracking:** Real-time status updates
- **Integration:** Link to inventory and delivery systems

### 4. **Retailer Onboarding**
- **Self-Registration:** Retailer provides shop details
- **Automatic Matching:** System suggests nearby distributors based on territory
- **Verification:** Distributor approves retailer
- **Activation:** Retailer gets access to company products

### 5. **Performance Management**
- **Salesman KPIs:** Visits, orders, revenue, retailers added, follow-ups
- **Distributor KPIs:** Order fulfillment, inventory turnover, retailer count
- **Company KPIs:** Total orders, revenue by region, new retailers, market share
- **Dashboard:** Real-time metrics with historical trends

### 6. **Credit & Payment Management**
- **Credit Limits:** Set by distributor per retailer
- **Payment Tracking:** Invoice-level payment status
- **Outstanding Balance:** Real-time calculation
- **Installments:** Support for partial payments
- **Payment Methods:** Cash, cheque, bank transfer, digital wallet

### 7. **Sample Request Workflow**
- **Request:** Retailer/Salesman requests product sample
- **Approval:** Company approves (tracks utilization)
- **Fulfillment:** Distributor ships sample
- **Tracking:** Delivery confirmation
- **Conversion:** Track sample → order conversion

### 8. **Competitor Intelligence**
- **Tracking:** Salesman reports competitor activities
- **Analysis:** Price monitoring, product comparisons, market trends
- **Dashboard:** Competitor heatmaps by region
- **Alerts:** Price changes, new competitors detected

### 9. **Advertising & Revenue Model**
- **Story Ads:** Premium companies can sponsor stories
- **Featured Products:** Products highlighted in retailer app
- **Promotional Banners:** Region-specific promotions
- **Revenue Sharing:** Platform takes 5-10% commission

### 10. **Notification System**
- **Types:** Order confirmations, payment reminders, performance alerts, story updates
- **Channels:** In-app, SMS, email, push notifications
- **Targeting:** Role-based and personalized

## Technical Architecture

### Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** Angular 22, Ionic 8, Capacitor 6
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** MongoDB 5.0+
- **Cache:** Redis
- **Storage:** AWS S3
- **Deployment:** AWS (EC2, RDS, CloudFront)
- **DevOps:** Docker, Kubernetes

### Architecture Pattern
- **Monorepo Structure** (Nx)
- **Shared Backend API**
- **Four Separate Frontend Apps**
- **Shared UI Component Library**
- **80-90% Code Reusability**

### Key Design Decisions
1. **Single Backend, Multiple Frontends** - Reduces complexity, ensures consistency
2. **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** - 6 roles: super-admin, company-admin, distributor-admin, distributor-staff, salesman, retailer
3. **Multi-Tenant Architecture** - Each company's data isolated
4. **Real-Time Capabilities** - WebSockets for notifications, orders
5. **Offline-First Mobile** - Salesmen app works without internet
6. **Geographic Matching** - Automatic distributor discovery based on coordinates
